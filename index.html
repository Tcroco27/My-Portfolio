— About

I build worlds with Unreal Engine 5. Across four years at York University I shipped eight games solo, in teams, and as the lead developer for two student studios published on Itch.io and Game Jolt.

My capstone, Cattleman's Crossing, was selected for the Level Up Showcase 2026 in Toronto, where I represented the team and demoed the game live. Before that I led a five-person team on a story-driven UE5 maze game, HorrorHugger, from concept through ship.

I'm comfortable in Blueprints and C++, fluent in Git workflows, and used to coordinating across designers, artists, and audio. I learn fast and I ship.
